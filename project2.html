Type Designers Exhibition

This project showcases the work of prominent type designers from around the world. I created an exhibition layout and branding materials that emphasize the beauty and versatility of typography.

The exhibition materials included posters, banners, and digital assets, all designed to highlight the creative process behind type design. The goal was to create a space where people could interact with the typefaces and understand the design behind them.
