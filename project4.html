Magazine Layout

This project focused on creating a sophisticated and visually appealing layout for a lifestyle magazine. I was responsible for designing the overall structure, typography, and visual elements to create an engaging reading experience.

The layout features a clean, minimalistic design with a focus on high-quality imagery and easy-to-read typography. The magazine’s goal was to inspire readers with articles about design, art, and culture.
