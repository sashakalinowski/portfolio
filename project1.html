United Nation Goals

This project was designed to visually communicate the global goals set by the United Nations. It includes a series of posters and digital assets to raise awareness on these important issues.

Created with a focus on clarity and impact, the goal was to design materials that could be easily shared and understood by a wide audience. This work was featured in several design exhibitions and is a representation of how design can be used to promote global change.
